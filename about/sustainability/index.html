SUSTAINABILITY

To give back to the wild places we love, we donate at least 1% of our sales to organizations dedicated to environmental conservation. We are proud use recycled and responsibly sourced materials to create our products.

Materials

Our paper goods

All of our prints and paper goods are printed on paper that maximizes recycled content and post-consumer waste, and with Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) certification and/or Green Seal certification. The cardstock for our prints and greeting cards comes from a paper mill powered 100% by wind energy. All of our prints, stationery and other paper goods are printed by us in our studio in Vermont.

Our tote bags and pouches

The fabric for our tote bags is a blend of 65% recycled cotton and 35% recycled post consumer poly. The recycled cotton is recuperated from fabric waste generated during the manufacturing process of new clothes, traditionally sent to landfills. The recycled poly is derived from 100% recycled PET plastic bottles. All our tote bags are Made in USA.

Packaging

Most of our packaging is plastic-free, and consists of recycled kraft paper or other recycled paper products. Our art prints are protected by an Eco Clear plant-based PLA sleeve that is certified compostable.

Get Inspired. Go Outside. & Give Back.

Support the wild places you love. Each year we donate a percentage of our profits to support environmental initiatives in Chile. Read more about the organizations we've donated to in the past.

Fundación Legado Chile

Non-profit organization that that creates, promotes and articulates natural heritage conservation projects to contribute to the sustainable development of Chile. To learn more about their mission, or to make a direct donation, check out their website.


Fundación Glaciares Chilenos

Non-profit organization that works in the preservation and protection of glaciers located in Chile, through the development of scientific research, visibility of content and creation of educational programs. To learn more about their mission, or to make a direct donation, check out their website.

Reforestemos Patagonia

Non-profit organization dedicated to recovering degraded ecosystems in Patagonia and other areas in Chile. Donations are used to plant native trees in regions with high ecological value throughout Patagonia and Chile. For more information about Fundación Reforestemos and their mission, or to make a direct donation, check out their website.

Paper goods + gifts for the adventurer at heart

At Soijen we are a creative duo that loves the outdoors. In the studio we play with a mix of hand + digital illustration techniques to make fun and bright designs that celebrate nature and wanderlust.

Through our designs we hope to bring you a smile - from nostalgia of a favorite paddling trip to inspiration for your next trail adventure.

To give back to the wild places we love, we donate at least 1% of our sales to organizations dedicated to environmental conservation. We are proud to partner with manufacturers that use sustainable production methods & responsibly sourced materials to create our products.

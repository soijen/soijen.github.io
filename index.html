welcome to sóijen

Patagonia is vast and wild. It can be tough to find information about places off the normal tourist routes.

At Sóijen, we are outdoor adventurers who connect fellow explorers to untapped regions of Patagonia.

We strive to work with travelers, agencies, and other lovers of Patagonia to promote and protect this one-of-a-kind region.

OUR STORY

We are Eduardo Donoso & Jess Young - co-owners and co-adventurers of Soijen. We started Soijen as a way to create designs that celebrate the natural beauty of Chilean Patagonia, our original home. Since moving to Vermont, our new playground in the Green Mountains has inspired us to continue creating outdoor-focused designs and gifts, to motivate fellow adventurers to get out and explore Patagonia, New England and beyond. At the core of all our products are sustainably sourced materials, and through partnerships with environmental non-profits we give back to the wild places we love.

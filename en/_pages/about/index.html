OUR STORY

We are Eduardo Donoso & Jess Young - co-owners and co-adventurers of Soijen Illustrations. Soijen was born in 2019 in Chilean Patagonia, when we started creating map illustrations of our favorite off-the-beaten-path spots that we explored in our camper van.

Some of our eclectic design inspirations and themes include: our favorite mountain peaks and ridgelines, minimalism, color pops, epic road trips, and all things kawaii. We now split our time between Vermont and Chilean Patagonia and you can find our products in shops across the US and Chile, online, and at many many many in-person events throughout the year in Vermont.

At the core of all our products are created from sustainably sourced materials, and through partnerships with conservation-focused non-profits we give back at least 1% of each purchase to the wild places we love.

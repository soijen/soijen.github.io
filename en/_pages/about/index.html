OUR STORY

Hi there! We are Eduardo & Jess, the creative duo behind Soijen. Through a mix of illustration techniques we create fun and bright designs that celebrate outdoor adventure and wanderlust.

Soijen was born in 2019 in Chilean Patagonia, when we started creating map illustrations of our favorite off-the-beaten-path spots that we explored in our camper van.

Some of our eclectic design inspirations and themes include: our favorite mountain peaks and ridgelines, minimalism, color pops, epic road trips, and all things kawaii. We now split our time between Vermont and Chilean Patagonia and you can find our products in shops across the US and Chile, online, and at many many many in-person events throughout the year.

To give back to the wild places we love, we donate at least 1% of our sales to organizations dedicated to environmental conservation. We are proud to use recycled and responsibly sourced materials to create our products.
